---
title: "Quiet systems scale well"
date: 2025-06-02
description: "How restrained design systems keep teams aligned."
tags: ["systems", "product"]
---

A restrained system is easy to grow. I start with three ingredients: a typographic rhythm, a small color set, and a comfortable spacing scale. When the basics are solid, teams can build fast without noise.

The bonus? Consistency feels premium.
